About VRIIC

Dr. Vidhya Research, Innovation and Incubation Centre established in the year 2018-19, motivates the students to bring out their innovative ideas. It aims to provide fund for each department with the concern of HoD’s approval. The best project will be selected by panel member in the corresponding department. Selected finalist will receive fund for their product. The project expo will be conducted by the Incubation Centre and best project from each department will be selected based on the new innovative ideas.

The centre also organizes startup competitions, hackathons, and mentoring programs, ensuring that innovators receive hands-on experience to refine their ideas.
