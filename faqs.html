Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and facilities

Student Programs

How can I apply for the Student Innovation Seed Fund?

To apply for the Student Innovation Seed Fund (SISF), follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form
  2. Submit your project proposal
  3. Attach necessary documentation
  4. Wait for the review committee's response

Applications are accepted throughout the academic year.

What types of projects are eligible for funding?

We fund innovative projects in various domains including:

  • Technology and Software Development
  • Renewable Energy Solutions
  • Healthcare Innovations
  • Agricultural Technology
  • Smart City Solutions

Faculty Programs

What is the Faculty Innovation Seed Fund?

The Faculty Innovation Seed Fund (FISF) is a funding program designed to support faculty members in their research and development initiatives. It provides financial assistance for:

  • Research Projects
  • Prototype Development
  • Patent Filing
  • Conference Presentations
How long is the funding period?

The funding period typically lasts for one academic year, with the possibility of extension based on project progress and requirements. Regular progress reports are required to maintain funding.

Facilities & Resources

What facilities are available for project work?

Our center offers various facilities including:

  • Research Laboratories
  • Prototyping Workshop
  • Computing Resources
  • Meeting Rooms
  • Conference Hall

All facilities are available during working hours and can be booked in advance.

How can I access the research facilities?

To access research facilities:

  1. Submit a facility access request form
  2. Get approval from your department head
  3. Complete the safety training (if required)
  4. Book the facility through our online portal
